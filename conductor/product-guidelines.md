# Product Guidelines: EshterakYar

## Design Philosophy
- **Custom/Branded:** The application will feature a unique, custom-designed interface that distinguishes it from standard Material Design apps, providing a distinct brand identity.

## Voice & Tone
- **Friendly and Helpful:** The application's copy will be approachable, encouraging, and focused on assisting the user, making financial management feel less daunting.

## Visual Identity
- **Brand Colors:** The design will utilize a specific brand color palette.
- **Dark Mode Strategy:** (Implied support based on modern standards, to be defined with brand colors).

## Accessibility
- **Custom Priority:** The application will prioritize accessibility features as defined by user feedback and specific use-case requirements.

## Localization
- **Persian (Farsi) Support:** The application will be fully localized for Persian users, including proper Right-to-Left (RTL) layout support and typography.
