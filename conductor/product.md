# Product Guide: EshterakYar

## # Initial Concept
EshterakYar is a comprehensive subscription management application designed to help users track, manage, and analyze their recurring expenses.

## Target Audience
- Individuals managing personal subscriptions (Spotify, Netflix, etc.)

## Core Goals
- Track payment due dates and amounts.
- Send renewal notifications/reminders.
- Analyze spending habits and total costs.

## Key Features
- **Subscription Management:** Manual entry of subscriptions including Name, Cost, Cycle, and Renewal Date.
- **Service Templates:** Pre-defined templates for popular services to speed up data entry.
- **Dashboard:** Visual dashboard providing monthly and yearly spending summaries.
- **Reminders:** Push notifications for upcoming payments to avoid missed due dates.
- **Currency Support:** Currency conversion capabilities for managing international subscriptions.

## Architecture & Data Strategy
- **Hybrid Model:** The application prioritizes an offline-first experience, storing data locally on the device, with optional cloud synchronization for users who wish to access their data across multiple devices.
